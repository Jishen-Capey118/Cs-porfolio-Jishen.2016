What is Vindinium???

"Vindinium can be defined as a game where one can code AI's and put them in a battle against many other bots. The simple goal of the game is to get more gold pieces than all the other bots. Your bot will gain gold during each turn accordingly with how many mines you have, for example, if you have 5 mines every turn you'll get 5 more gold pieces. However, others can also steal your mines. This is done either by simply going to a bot's mine and taking it, or by killing the owner of the mine. If you are killed you will lose all your mines and the mines will go to that of killer's ownership. Also, if each bot has around 100 health, attacking another mine mine will remove 20 from one's health, and so will attacking another bot. Additionally, each turn you will lose one health. To heal yourself without dying you must go to a tavern. You can pay 2 gold at a tavern in order to be healed for 50 health. That's the basic gist of the game!"
