-About Me-

1-Name: Jishen Prakash

2-Birth: December 18, 2000

3-Age: 15 years old

4-School: Summit Rainier

My name is Jishen Prakash and I am an Indian Fijian American. My ancestors were originally from India. However, because of the British occupation in India many Indians were scattered across the globe to be put to labor. In the 19th century my great grandfather left all his families in a boat to the island of Fiji (off the coast of Australia). From then my family heritage ran in Fiji until 1987 when my parents came to America.
As an American, I joined Boy Scouts in the year of 2011. It was a life changing even, because I learned a lot about life and how things work. I learned to work hard and to keep fighting on. I also learned first aid, and Wilderness Survival. I am currently working on my life ranking to become an eagle.
I also love Soccer. My like Manchester United, PSG, Borussia Dortmund, Inter Milan, but best of all BARCELONA!!! Soccer is a major part of my life and it makes me feel safe.
I have been playing the flute since 2014. In my opinion the flute is my favorite instrument. It is very harmonious and calming. I enjoy playing it in front of crowds. My flute changed my life.
In 2008, while we were at home, our kind Neighbor gave us a box. Inside that box there was a rat-terrier Chihuahua. We named her Princess Prakash! Today princess is 8 years old and she enjoys life.
Chess is my favorite board game. I started playing chess five years ago, and I have loved it since then. Without Chess the world would suck badly. Chess is not just a game it is a WAR and Battle of strategy. It helps us plan out for battles to save our beloved Kings and queens!
I started watching anime in 2006 and since then Anime has given me chills to my spine. I have seen maybe one hundred different series. I love Black butler and Prison School even though they are completely overrated by many Otakus.
